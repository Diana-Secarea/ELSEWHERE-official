About Elsewhere

A new way to experience the world's most interesting cities.

At Elsewhere, we believe travel should be more than just visiting places – it should be about living different lives. Our curated character experiences let you step into lifestyles you've always been curious about.

Each "Elsewhere" character is carefully crafted by locals and experts to give you an authentic immersion into a way of life. From cooking in Bangkok's hidden alleys to surfing at sunrise in Lisbon, these experiences go beyond typical tourism.

Choose a character that speaks to you, and for a weekend or week, live as if you were truly elsewhere.
